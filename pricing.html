Frequently Asked Questions

How do I go from Free to Standard?

As soon as you hit 100 packages, you can't add further until you connect your credit card.

Is there a time limit to the 100 packages?

Nope. It can take you 100 years to finish up the free 100 packages and it will still be free.

I have a lot of packages in my current system, how do I migrate?

It's really a case-to-case basis. Contact us and we'll take a look at your system. We can create a separate page to track old numbers.

When do I get billed?

You get billed every 30 days and the number of packages you created will be determined by that.

Can I get annual discount?

Yes, but it's not yet built into the system. Contact us for negotiations and special packages.

Any other fees for the standard plan?

No, there are no additional fees. We don't do hidden fees here.
